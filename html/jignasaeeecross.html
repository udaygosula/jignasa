Rounds

Round 1
1The selected teams from ROUND-1 will move to ROUND -2.
All the necessary Electrical and Electronic components like diodes, resistors, capacitors, ICs , etc., will be given in ROUND -2.
The teams have to create a working model with the components given to them.

Round 2
In this round the participants are shown pictures using the projector in which there will be some non-technical objects with the operators in between them per slide, and the team must guess an electrical object with them. The participants will be shown 25 slides with a specific time limit per slide. The team which answers maximum questions correct will be shortlisted to the final round

Round 3
This is a puzzle round in which the pictures related to electrical and technology are given to the teams which are cut in to 9 squares and are jumbled up. The team which identifies the correct picture first is declared as the winner of the event.

Rules

1. Team should be registered for JIGNASA-2018.
2. Students from different colleges can form a team
3. Arguments are not entertained.
4. All the participants will be awarded with a participation certificate.
5. Winners will grab prize money.
In case of tie, one more Question Set (Containing 10 questions, duration 10 minutes, Max. Marks 10 will be given to candidates. Winner will be declared based on the result of above question set.

Judging Criteria

The one who has mastery of all rounds is destined as the winner.
