Description

Do you have enough knowledge about science and technology?? Quiz is a competition regarding the general concepts & technical innovations in their respective branches.
If you have the knowledge and want to prove to the world that you do!!
The stage is set to provide you with a chance to showcase your grey cells' worth in whichever field your work is best in. So come hither, challenge your self.


Judgement Criteria

The one who has mastery of all rounds is destined as the winner. In case of tie, One more Question Set (Containing 10 questions, duration 10 minutes, Max. Marks 10)will be given to candidates. Winner will be declared based on the result of above question set.

Rules

1. Maximum of 3 members per team. Event consists of 3 rounds.
2. Arguments at any cost are not entertained.
3. All the participants will be awarded with a participation certificate.
4. Winners will grab prize money.



Note: In case of tie, One more Question Set (Containing 10 questions, duration 10 minutes, Max. Marks 10)will be given to candidates.
Winner will be declared based on the result of above question set.
