Rules

1. Only group participation is allowed. Each group consists of maximum 3 members.
2. The contestants will be given contact details of 3 club members (the number is three so that there is no delay in the proceedings of the competition in case a lot of answers start coming at the same time).
3. A clue will be given & Upon solving that clue the contestants have to send the answer to the numbers provided via Whatsapp. If the answer is correct then the volunteer(s) shall give them the location where they may find the next clue.
4. There will be another volunteer present at the location who'll ask one general basic question to the contestant group and later the next clue will be given.
5. This continues till the penultimate volunteer gives the location of the final destination.
6. Mobile phones are prohibited.
7. Communication with opponent team is strictly prohibited.
8. Violating of rules will results in the elimination of team.

Description

A brief contest to measure growth in technical abilities or skill for hungry brains.



Judgement Criteria

Time taken to reach the final location will be considered .
The team which breaks all the clues earlier will be the wins.

3 (Maximum)
