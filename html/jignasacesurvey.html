Format

Round 1
Screening test: Comprising of both objective and subjective questions on conventional and advanced surveying.

Round 2
Final round will be descriptive round. Participants should find solutions to the given problems pertaining to survey in the given duration of time. Knowledge on usage of surveying equipment is a prerequisite.

You need
Surveying skills, calculator and safe hands to handle the equipment.

Rules

1. This is a team event.
2. Team may consist of 2 or 3 members only.
3. Written test will be conducted as a screening test if the teams are more.

Description

The participants will be asked to solve a field problem in the final round related to surveying using the surveying instruments like chain, tape, compass, dumpy level and theodolite only.

Terms and Conditions

Participants are soley responsible for any damages of given instruments.

Judgement Criteria

1. Knowledge of Proper use of surveying equipment.
2. Speed and efficiency of completing the given task.
3. Proper representation of Bookwork of the problem statement.
4. In case of tie the preliminary round scores will also be taken into account.


Team size
2 or 3 (Maximum)

50/- per head
