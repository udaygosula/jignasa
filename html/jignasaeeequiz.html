Rounds

Screening Test
1. Students should form teams of their own consisting of 3 members.
2. Each team must go through a screening test for 30 minutes.
3.Top 9 teams will be selected for the next round.

Manupulation Round
1. Questions will be asked for each team.
2. Options will be provided for each question.
3. Students should answer the question within 30 seconds.
Top 6 teams will be selected for the next round.

Kismat Connection
1. Questions will be asked for each team.
2. Negative marks.
3. Students should answer the question within 30 seconds.
4. Top 3 teams will be selected for the final round.

Do or Die
1. This is a buzzer round.
2. The team which gives their response first will be given chance to answer.
3. Negative marks.
4. The team which secured highest score will be declared as the winner team.

Rules

1. Any kind of misbehavior/malpractice is not encouraged.
2. Certificate will be issued to each and every participant.
3. Students from different colleges can form a team.
4. In case of any arguments/disputes, the decision made by judges / coordinators will be final.

Description

A brief questionnaire to measure growth in knowledge abilities or skill for hungry brains. The one who has the mastery of all zones of technical knowledge is decided to be the winner.


Judgement Criteria

The team which stands first at the end of the last round will be declared as the "winner".
