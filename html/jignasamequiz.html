Rules

1. Event consists of 3 rounds.
2. Arguments at any cost are not entertained.
3. Spot entries are allowed.
4. Two participants per team.
5. Prelims will be conducted.
6. Any act against the spirit of the game will lead to disqualification of the team.

Judgement Criteria

1. The one who has mastery of all rounds is destined as the winner.
2. In case of tie, one more question set (containing 10 questions, duration 10 minutes max. Marks (10) will be given to candidates.
3. Winner will be declared based on the result of above question set.
4. The team which secured highest score will be declared as the winner team.

Description

Do you have enough knowledge about science and technology??

If you have the knowledge and want to prove to the world that you do!!
The stage is set to provide you with a chance to showcase your grey cells’ worth in whichever field you are best in. so come, challenge yourself.
A brief questionnaire to measure the growth in knowledge and skill for hungry brain. The one who has the mastery of all zones of technical knowledge is decided to be the winner.
