Description

Prepare your gear for a hunt. Dress yourself with calmness. Make your weapons(minds) sharp. Be a Sherlock Holmes in decoding the clues. Complete the quest as fast as possible to calm your hunger with a winner satisfaction.

The teams will be given a clue at the start of the game. The clue leads to next clue and a component of a circuit. Succesively this process goes on leading to a circuit that can be designed with all the components that a team has with them.



Rules

1.Team limit is two members.
2. Use of mobile phones and any sources of knowledge(books etc.,) are strictly prohibited.
3. The team which completes the quest first wil be the winners.
4.The coordinator can change the rules of the game depending on the situation.
5. On spot registrations is also available.
