Rules

1. Participants must be from ECE/EEE stream.
2. Students from different colleges can form a team.
3. Every team has to register in JIGNASA-2018 registration page.
4. The decision of judges shall be treated as final.
5. The use of mobile or any other device to get the needed information either from internet or through text books will lead to disqualification.
6. The event managers can change the rules depending upon the situation and in such case the participants will be given prior information.
7. Any communication between two teams will lead to disqualification of both the teams.
8. All the components mentioned in the problem statement (theme) will be provided.

The finalists will be selected, based on the efficiency of the output and the time taken to make the best possible circuit.
