Rounds

Phase :1
1. Students should form teams of their own consisting of 2 members.
2. Each team must go through a screening test for 30 minutes.
3. Top 10 teams will be selected for the next phase.
Phase :2
1. Pictures will be shown to team, they must answer to questions presented in pictures
2. Top 5 teams will be selected for the next round
Rapid Fire Round
1. The team with the quick response , will be given a chance to answer.
2. The team securing the highest score will be declared as the winner team.

Description

A brief questenario to measure growth in knowledge abilities or skill for hungry brains. The one who has the mastery of all zones of technical knowledge is decided to be the winner.

Rules

1. Any kind of misbehavior/malpractice is not encouraged.
2. Certificate will be issued to each and every participant.
3. Students from different colleges can form a team.
4. In case of any arguments/disputes, the decision made by judges / coordinators will be final.
