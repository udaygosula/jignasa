Description

Of course, soccer is a beloved sport for many of us. Now its time for our robos to transmigrate with soccer legends like Messy,Ronaldo to rule the reign of soccer field.
In this game, participants should hit the ball into the opponents goal to score points. Participants can hit as many goals as possible in limited time. Goal posts will be on either side of the field.

Judgement Criteria

The one who has mastery of all rounds is destined as the winner.



Rules

1. Team can have maximum number of three members.
2.Robo may be wired or wireless
3. Only one participant should control the robot no player can be exchanged in the middle of match.
4. A robot can push or hit the ball. It cannot withhold or grab the ball.
5. Human interference (e.g. touching the robot) during the game is not allowed .
6.The coordinator can change the rules of the game depending on the situation.
7. On spot registrations are also available.
