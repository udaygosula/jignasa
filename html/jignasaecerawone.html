Description

It is not how you start the race or where you are during the race, it is how you cross the finish line that will matter. Let your car drink the power. Let your wheels speak on the track. Will see who will emerge as 'the king of the track.
The contestants should build their RC (remote controlled) cars. The car should pass through the track provided. The car which finishes the race in less time crossing through the obstacles will be the winners of the race.

Judgement Criteria

The one who has mastery of all rounds is destined as the winner.

Rules

1. Team members are limited to two.
2. Mobile phones should not be used.
3. Talking to others is not entertained.
4. No other materials should be used except the one provided.
5. The coordinator can change the rules of the game depending on the situation.
6. On the spot registrations is available.
